# shoot
Used for determining the result of a ranged attack
Based on a D20 roll with a default DC of 11

+/- raises/lowers your DC respectively (so + is actually detrimental).
Advantage and disadvantage make you roll more times. Under advantage, you take the highest roll; under disadvantage, you take the lowest.

The **o**pen flag should be used on all shots, especially if no other flags are active.

Flags:
- **A**dvantage
- **r**apid fire (advantage, +3)
- **F**anning (advantage, +4)
- **b**ehind wall (disadvantage)
- **B**lind (disadvantage)
- **D**isadvantage
- **e**xplosive (-4)
- **n**ear (-4)
- **d**istracted (-3)
- **s**plash damage (-3)
- **l**ong charge (-2)
- **o**pen (0)
- fluid **a**bsorption (+1)
- **L**ow projectile speed (+3)
- **v**ision difficulty (+3)
- **f**ar (+5)
